## üîç Key Insights Discovered

### Demand Patterns
- **Peak Hours**: Morning (8-10 AM) and Evening (6-8 PM) show highest demand
- **Consistent Weekday Demand**: No significant variation across weekdays
- **Supply-Side Issues**: Driver cancellations exceed customer cancellations

### Revenue Analysis
- **UPI Dominance**: Primary payment method driving 60%+ of transactions
- **Vehicle Performance**: Go Sedan and Premier Sedan show highest completion rates
- **Route Concentration**: Top 10 routes drive 40%+ of total revenue

### Operational Insights
- **Revenue Leakage**: Peak hours show highest revenue loss due to cancellations
- **Driver Optimization**: High-demand routes need targeted driver allocation
- **Customer Behavior**: Payment method preferences directly impact revenue
